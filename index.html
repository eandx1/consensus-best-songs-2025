About

The Cultural Record

A data-driven consensus of 2025's musical landscape.

Every December, the music press releases hundreds of "Best of" lists. Consensus Best Songs 2025 acts as a meta-critic, aggregating these diverse viewpoints into a single, transparent ranking. We identify the songs that defined the year, whether through sheer ubiquity or intense critical obsession.
